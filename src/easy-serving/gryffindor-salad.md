# Gryffindor Salad


## info
* Source: Katie Jones

## ingredients
* romaine lettuce
* corn
* tomatoes
* pepitas
* big couscous (whole wheat)
* shredded parmesan cheese
* creamy savory basil dressing (try taste of home)

## method
* Combine ingredients in bowl.
* Serve with dressing.

## settings/pairings
Creamy Basil Dressing
